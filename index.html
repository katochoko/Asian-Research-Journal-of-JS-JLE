About the Journal

This journal is an academic publication aimed at supporting the research activities of scholars worldwide who focus on Japanese studies and Japanese language education in Asia, and disseminating their findings to the world.

Our primary objective is to serve as a "stepping stone" for researchers aiming for high-standard international academic journals, improving the quality of research through the publication process and contributing to the future career development of contributors.

This journal particularly welcomes submissions from early-career researchers and graduate students engaged in Japanese and Asian studies.

The journal publishes original research in the fields of Japanese Studies and Japanese Language Education, primarily focusing on East Asia (including Japan) and Southeast Asia.

Recognizing that Japanese Studies and Japanese Language Education are inextricably linked in both academic and institutional contexts outside of Japan, this journal provides a platform for integrating insights from both fields.

By covering both areas, we aim to stimulate exchange between researchers, creating new perspectives and advancing academic development regarding "Japan."

Submission Guidelines

  • Format: Peer-reviewed (Double-blind peer review).
  • Publication frequency: Planned annually (subject to change depending on operational conditions).
  • Languages: Japanese or English.
  • Nature of Manuscripts: Limited to unpublished, complete manuscripts. Simultaneous submission to other journals is not permitted.
  • Student Submissions: Junior researchers, such as graduate students, should consult with and have their manuscripts refined by a researcher holding a doctoral degree before submission.
  • Medium: Online journal (Open Access). No print edition.
  • Types of Manuscripts: Research articles only. Other types of submissions, such as research notes or book reviews, are not accepted at this stage.
  • Support Fees:

    These fees are intended solely to cover the actual costs of peer review management, editorial work, and website maintenance, and have no influence on the academic evaluation of submitted manuscripts.

    • Upon Submission: 25 USD (Preliminary review and peer review administration fee. Non-refundable even if the manuscript is not accepted.)

    • Upon Acceptance: 25 USD (Editing and website maintenance fee. Non-refundable.)
      For submission from Vietnam: 250,000 VND
    • Total: 50 USD (500,000 VND)

Submission and Publication Process

  1. Preparation: Prepare a complete manuscript in accordance with the style guidelines.
  2. Submission: Send the manuscript via email to the designated address.
  3. Payment of Review Fee: After submission, authors will be instructed by the editorial office to pay the first installment.
  4. Preliminary Review: The editorial board will check the theme, format, ethics, and academic standard.
  5. Peer Review (Double-blind): Manuscripts that pass the preliminary review will undergo anonymous review by at least two external reviewers and editorial board members.
  6. Final Decision: The editorial board will make the final decision based on the review results.
  7. Payment of Publication Fee: If accepted, authors will be instructed by the editorial office to pay the second installment.
  8. Publication: The paper will be published in the online journal.

Copyright and Open Access

  • Open Access: To promote the free exchange of academic knowledge, this journal adopts an open access model where all content is made available to the public immediately.
  • Copyright: Copyright of the published paper belongs to the author.
  • Grant of Rights: The author grants the journal permission to publish the paper in other media with proper attribution to the author(s) and utilize it for the journal's activities free of charge. Upon acceptance, a designated agreement form must be submitted.

Ethical Standards

This journal places a strong emphasis on research ethics and strictly prohibits plagiarism, fabrication, falsification, and inappropriate use of artificial intelligence in research and manuscript preparation.

Violations of research ethics may result in rejection, retraction, or other appropriate measures as determined by the editorial board.
